About the Event

What is
AVATAR'26?

AVATAR'26 is the annual technical fest of Rajkiya Engineering College, Banda — a two-day battleground where students compete, innovate, and reverberate. 12 challenges. One arena. Survive to innovate.

From robotics to coding, puzzle solving to creative design — AVATAR'26 brings together the brightest minds across disciplines to compete in a thrilling, Squid Game-style challenge format. Each event is a chance to prove your skills, earn glory, and claim your place on the leaderboard.

The Games

Choose Your Battle

12 events across technology, engineering, creativity, and wit. Each game is a test of a different skill. Which one will you survive?

Schedule

Event Timeline

Two days packed with challenges. Breaks at 1:20–2:00 PM (Lunch) and 5:00–5:15 PM daily. Plan your survival carefully.

○ DAY 1 — 7 APRIL
9:00 AM
Inauguration & Opening
Main Hall — Welcome address, AVATAR'26 rules briefing. All participants mandatory.
10:00 AM
1. Cubic Puzzle
Main Hall — Speed-solve the Rubik's Cube. Average of 5 solves determines rank. Blind solve bonus available.
11:00 AM
2. Stick Build
Workshop Hall — Build the tallest free-standing structure using sticks & glue. 1.5 hrs. Weight test at end.
11:30 AM
3. Poster Design
Computer Lab — Theme revealed at start. Design A3 poster in 2 hours using any digital tool. Original work only.
12:00 PM
4. Prompt Engineering
Computer Lab — 5 rounds of AI prompt challenges. Live final round demo to judges. Creativity meets precision.
1:20 PM — 2:00 PM
☕ LUNCH BREAK
All events pause. Report back by 2:00 PM sharp.
2:00 PM
5. Circuit Craze
Electronics Lab — Build & test circuits from given components within 2 hours. Live judge test at submission.
3:30 PM
6. Robo Race
Ground Floor Arena — Custom robots race through obstacle course. 2 trial runs per team. Bot inspection 30 min prior.
4:30 PM
7. Treasure Hunt
Entire Campus — First clue released to all teams simultaneously. Teams must stay together throughout the hunt.
5:00 PM — 5:15 PM
🍵 SHORT BREAK
15-minute refreshment break. All events resume at 5:15 PM.
7:30 PM
⬛ Day 1 Closes
Venue closes. Rest up — Day 2 begins at 9:00 AM. Survive to innovate.
△ DAY 2 — 8 APRIL
9:00 AM
Day 2 Briefing
Main Hall — Final rules review. Venue assignments for all Day 2 events distributed to participants.
9:30 AM
1. Ideathon
Seminar Hall — Theme revealed, prepare 10-slide PPT. 8-min pitch + 2-min Q&A. Judged on creativity & feasibility.
11:00 AM
2. Tower of Hanoi
Computer Lab — Solve puzzles from 3 to 7 discs. Speed & accuracy determine rank. 30-sec penalty per illegal move.
11:45 AM
3. CodeCraft
Computer Lab — 3-hour competitive programming contest. C, C++, Java, Python. No internet. Most problems solved wins.
1:20 PM — 2:00 PM
☕ LUNCH BREAK
All events pause. Return to venues by 2:00 PM sharp.
2:00 PM
4. Rocket Launcher
Open Ground — Build & launch water-pressure rockets. 3 attempts per team. Goggles mandatory. Accuracy earns bonus.
3:30 PM
5. Egg Drop Challenge
Open Ground — Build protective casing then drop from 5m. Height increases until one team's egg survives. Goggles on.
5:00 PM — 5:15 PM
🍵 SHORT BREAK
Pre-ceremony refreshment. All participants to Main Hall by 5:15 PM.
5:15 PM
Results Compilation & Judging
All event judges submit final scores. Overall leaderboard prepared for the Grand Finale.
6:00 PM
🏆 Grand Finale & Prize Distribution
Main Hall — All 12 event winners announced. Certificates and trophies distributed. Closing ceremony of AVATAR'26.
6:45 PM
Group Photo & Felicitation
Open Ground — Official group photograph of all participants, volunteers & faculty. Medal & certificate photo session.
7:30 PM
🎆 AVATAR'26 — End
The games are over. The survivors are crowned. See you next year. ○ △ □
